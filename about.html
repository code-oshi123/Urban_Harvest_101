Our Story

About Urban Harvest

We're on a mission to make sustainable living accessible to everyone through fresh, organic produce and eco-friendly practices.

Our Mission

Urban Harvest was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable food system that benefits both people and the planet.

We partner with over 200 local farms to deliver fresh, organic produce while reducing carbon emissions and supporting local economies.

Every box we deliver represents our commitment to transparency, quality, and environmental responsibility.

Our Eco-Friendly Practices

How we make a difference every day

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

All deliveries use electric vehicles and optimized routes to minimize our carbon footprint.

Reusable Packaging

100% compostable or reusable packaging. Return boxes for credits on your next order.

Water Conservation

Our partner farms use drip irrigation and water-saving techniques to reduce waste.

Organic Certification

All products are certified organic, free from harmful pesticides and chemicals.

Fair Trade Practices

We ensure farmers receive fair compensation for their sustainable practices.

Local Sourcing

All produce comes from farms within a 50-mile radius, supporting local economies.
