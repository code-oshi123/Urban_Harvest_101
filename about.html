About Urban Harvest

Making sustainable living accessible to everyone through fresh, organic produce and eco-friendly practices.

Our Mission

Urban Harvest was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable food system that benefits both people and the planet.

We partner with over 200 local farms to deliver fresh, organic produce while reducing carbon emissions and supporting local economies.

Every box we deliver represents our commitment to transparency, quality, and environmental responsibility.
